Private Jewish Heritage & Old Town Orientation Tour

A walking tour with your local guide takes you through Old Town to Zudioska ulica (Jewish street). Here, in the heart of Dubrovnik, you will find Europe's oldest functioning Sephardic Synagogue and second oldest in the world. Damaged over time but still stands there and tells us a great story about life of Jewish citizens who inhabited Dubrovnik in medieval time. Their coexistence with local citizens, Dubrovnik Republic government, their special skills. We'll visit the Synagogue on the top of the building and museum on a floor below with some ancient artifacts, Torah, documents. . . The rest of the Old city won't be forgotten as we are going to stroll through its narrow streets that are whispering about secrets behind walls. Comfortable shoes, a hat, and a bottle of water are recommended.

No matter if we start from Pile or Ploce gate, after introduction about Dubrovnik's timeline, we'll pass next to: -Sponza Palace - City bank, mint, customs, short term storages - over Placa street to Jewish street ( Zudioska ulica ) - Prijeko street - Franciscan monastery ( Old pharmacy ) - Big Onofrio's fountain - Pile gate where still stands Jewish fountain - Visit Synagogue and of course if you have some additional wishes regarding tour I am always ready to adjust it to your preferences.

Private Half Day Tour around Mighty Walls of Dubrovnik

On this tour we are climbing on Dubrovnik's City wall. It is a 2 kilometers ( 1, 3 miles ) walk that takes some effort due to stairs on our way. There are 3 entrances so we can do the whole circle or approx half of it. It is worth seeing Dubrovnik from a completely different perspective where Old town looks like on the palm of your hand. At a couple of windows, there are hidden parts of Jewish tombstones used for reconstruction by Napoleon's army. However, as a local guide I am, I know where they are. Even if we make half or the whole wall tour we shall see some other main sites as Onofrio's Fountain, Franciscan monastery, Rectors palace, Cathedral. A hat, bottle of water and some comfortable shoes are recommended.

No matter if we start from Pile or Ploce gate, after climbing on a wall and short introduction to Dubrovnik's history we'll walk the wall and after we get off from it there are many interesting buildings and monuments to see as: - Domenican monastery -Sponza Palace ( bank, mint, treasury ) - main, Placa street ( for locals Stradun ) -St. Blase church ( patron saint of Dubrovnik ) -Rector's Palace ( the Seat of Dubrovnik Republic ) - Old city port, Cathedral - Franciscan monastery ( Old pharmacy ) - Big Onofrio's fountain - City wall Our tour can finish at the same meeting point where we started.

Dubrovnik's War for Peace Tour

We shall start the tour driving in direction of mountain Srđ. The place where the biggest battle for Dubrovnik and its citizens took part and where Museum of Homeland war is situated. As beeing one of volunteers in City defense I can tell you some situations from my personal experience. The stuning, breathtaking view will open in front of you . We'll make a few short stops on our way so you can take some nice and interesting photos with you. After visiting museum we'll proceed to the Old city of Dubrovnik to get further back in the past, at the beginning of existence of today's Dubrovnik. The timeline will guide us through centuries up to today. Unfortunately, still there are war marks made by shrapnels, scars that testify sad days of our recent past.
